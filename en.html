About Us

DGTU Tekton Engineering LLC was founded in 2012 with the goal of contributing to a better society for future generations in the field of civil engineering.

The company specialises in providing expert services for infrastructure projects. Our aim is to proactively address the complex challenges that affect built structures and to develop effective solutions.

Mission

Continuous professional development and resolving complex engineering challenges.

Vision

To develop methods that keep us one step ahead of unforeseen challenges.

Motto

“Continuous improvement is the key to success.”

What We Offer

Services

Design of Infrastructure Works

Preparation of complete design and technical documentation for road, utility, and other infrastructure in accordance with applicable regulations and standards.

Revision of Infrastructure Works

Expert review of design documentation for infrastructure projects, with detailed analysis and recommendations for compliance.

Expert Witnessing for Infrastructure Works

Preparation of expert findings and opinions for the needs of court proceedings in the field of civil infrastructure engineering.

Field Investigations & Laboratory Testing

Geomechanical field investigations and laboratory material testing to support the design of infrastructure projects.

Supervision of Infrastructure Works

Professional construction supervision during the construction and reconstruction of infrastructure works, with quality and compliance control.

Engineering Geodesy

Geodetic surveys and measurements for the design, construction, and monitoring of infrastructure works.
